The House On The Water

still as the roaring seas

safe as the dead of night

the house on the water

joy like a broken heart

calm as a wounded animal

the house on the water

peace calls to fear

and the arms of the abyss reach out longingly

anguish beckons as a friend long lost

yet to the house must I cling

the house on the water
